About me

Sander van Cranenburgh is Associate Professor in the Transport and Logistics Group of the faculty of Technology Policy and Management of Delft University of Technology.


Sander his research focusses on choice behaviour analysis. During his Postdoc he worked on Random Regret Minimization (RRM) based discrete choice models, and made several important methodological contributions to this modelling approach. His current research increasingly focusses on enriching understanding of (travel) choice behaviour through developing new data-driven modelling approaches. In this research he specifically seeks the edge between traditional theory-driven approaches, such as Discrete Choice Models, and data-driven approaches, such as Artificial Neural Networks.
